About the Faculty of Information Science (FIS)

The Faculty of Information Science (FIS) at Universiti Teknologi MARA (UiTM) plays an important role in nurturing digital talent aligned with Malaysia’s fast-growing technology landscape. Through high-quality academic programmes, industry-driven curriculum, and experienced lecturers, the faculty prepares students to excel in fields such as data analytics, information systems, multimedia, and information management.

FIS continues to strengthen its position through collaboration with government, industry partners, and international institutions. These efforts ensure that students are equipped with relevant skills, future-ready competencies, and strong professional values that meet global standards.

With a vibrant learning environment supported by innovative facilities, FIS is committed to developing graduates who are knowledgeable, ethical, and capable of contributing to national development and the global digital economy.
